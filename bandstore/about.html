Merle Haggard

ABOUT

Merle Haggard is one of Countries All Time Greatest Artist. He was a part of over 35 #1 hits during his illustrious decade long career. Many Country singers still talk about him as their inspiration.


But not only was it country singers but a lot of rock bands during his heydey talked about him being an inspiration. One of the pioneers of outlaw country along with his fellow bandmates in the Highwaymen Merle will alway be remembered.

Born in Oildale, CA in 1930 during the great depression and living until April 6, 2016 saw a lot of change and spoke to the way a lot of middle America felt.
We will never see another like him and are lucky his music is still with us today.

Merle Haggard
